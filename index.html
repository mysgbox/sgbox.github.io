SGbox – Smart Grid Box

From a connected home to an intelligent neighborhood — with SGbox, energy becomes collective.

See the Prototype

Problem & Solution

The Current Problem

  • Wasted Energy: In Morocco, surplus solar energy is often lost as storage solutions are rare and expensive.
  • Lack of Coordination: There is no energy coordination between neighbors, which hinders autonomy and the valorization of local renewable resources.

Our Solution: SGbox

  • Open-Source Smart Box: SGbox transforms each house into an active node of a local mini-grid.
  • Energy Sharing: It optimizes production, consumption, storage, and enables electricity sharing between neighbors for collective and sustainable energy.

How It Works

1. Data Collection

Measures electrical and environmental parameters and integrates local weather forecasts.

2. Smart Analysis

a locally trained llm and EMHASS enable contextual decision-making and predict production/consumption.

3. Optimization & Control

Node-RED orchestrates real-time load scheduling and energy sharing between homes and handelling parsing and data logging.

4. User Interface

A fully customizable Home Assistant platform combining precision monitoring with secure, global remote control.

System Architecture

Supervision

Raspberry Pi running Node-RED, Home Assistant, and a local LLM for orchestration.

Measurements

ESP32 devices with shunt/hall-effect sensors for telemetry and local control.

Hybrid storage

Features a primary Li-Ion battery bank and an experimental thermal storage unit.

Local Intelligence

EMHASS and a lightweight LLM for short-term production and consumption forecasting.

Validation & Replication

Validation Methodology

  1. End-to-end telemetry integrity tests (Sensor → Cloud).
  2. Coordination drills verifying energy sharing between nodes.
  3. Storage cycling assessment for battery and thermal reservoirs.
  4. Resilience testing for power-loss recovery and network partitions.

Impact, Innovation & Vision

Ecological Impact

Reduces energy waste and enhances solar production with open-source, repairable hardware.

Technological Innovation

A novel fusion of IoT, local AI (cloud-free), and predictive optimization in a distributed, collaborative architecture.

Social Impact

Empowers neighborhood energy autonomy and democratizes technology through an open-source, participatory approach.

Our Long-Term Vision

To establish SGbox as a Moroccan open-source standard for smart microgrids, contributing to a local, sustainable, and citizen-centric energy transition.
